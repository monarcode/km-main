About Us

Remember that savings box or piggy bank you had as a child? We made it into an app! At KoloMoni,we're a team of young people who understand how difficult it is to save money especially with today's economic pressures. This is why we have created a Mobile and Web app to ensure for fast, convenient and seamless savings.
